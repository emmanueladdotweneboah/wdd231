Occupational Therapy Hub - Site Plan

WDD 231 Individual Website Project

Site Name

Occupational Therapy Hub

This name was selected because it clearly represents a central online resource for occupational therapy services, education, rehabilitation resources, and patient support.

Optional Domain: occupationaltherapyhub.org

Site Purpose

Occupational Therapy Hub provides educational information, therapy resources, rehabilitation guidance, caregiver support materials, and contact services for individuals interested in occupational therapy. The website helps users understand therapy interventions and access professional support.

Services Included

  • Occupational Therapy Information
  • Rehabilitation Resources
  • Therapy Programs
  • Caregiver Support
  • Appointment Requests
  • Educational Articles

Scenarios

  • How can occupational therapy help a child improve daily living skills?
  • Where can I contact an occupational therapist or schedule an appointment?
  • What therapy services are available after a stroke or injury?
  • What resources are available for caregivers supporting individuals with disabilities?

Color Scheme

  • Sea Green (#2E8B57) – Headings, navigation, buttons.
  • Light Gray (#F4F4F4) – Page background and content areas.
  • Steel Blue (#4682B4) – Links, accents, borders, highlights.

These colors create a calm, professional, healthcare-focused appearance.

Typography

  • Poppins – Headings and navigation.
  • Open Sans – Body text and forms.
  • Merriweather – Quotes and featured content.
